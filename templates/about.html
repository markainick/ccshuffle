Creative Commons shuffle

Search for creative commons music all over the web and listen or download songs, which you like, instantly.

Find Out More

Enjoy the world of creative commons music !

Creative commons shuffle gives you the possibility to search for creative commons music on the internet. You can listen to the music, favorite the music and download it instantly. It is a Spotify of creative commons music - simply better, because you can feel free to share the music to your friends and use it in your projects.
